Places to Visit in Kedarnath

Temple

Kedarnath Temple

1 km from City Center
One of the most sacred temples and holiest Hindu pilgrimages in India. Dedicated to Lord Shiva.
Hamlet

Sonprayag

13 km from City Center
A tiny hamlet on the confluence banks of rivers Mandakini and Basuki.
Lake

Vasuki Tal

22 km from City Center
A lake at an altitude of 4135 meters offering a view of the neighbouring Chaukhamba peaks.
Temple

Adi Shankaracharya Samadhi

1 km from City Center
Resting place of Sri Sankaracharya who attained Nirvana at age 32 here.
Temple

Triyuginarayan

At Kedarnath Temple
The place where the wedding of Lord Shiva and Parvati took place.
